2. How important internet nowadays?

Nowadays, the internet is very important because networks and devices can connect or communicate with each other regardless of location or distance. Because of how the internet creates connections between people of many backgrounds, users can easily access, receive, or send information and services to and from users and sources that are not only locally found, but are also found in foreign areas.


3. Can you live without internet?

It depends on the user and what purpose individuals or groups possess when using the internet. In many cases, it would be impossible to live without internet if it was used for other purposes aside from work, study, or research.
